Help Page

Here are some tips to help you use taxi Ranker:

  1. Firstly you need to make sere your cellphone's GPS location is on when you are using this app.
  2. Click on the "Find taxi Ranks" button
  3. When asked to,click on "Share location".There may be a slight delay
  4. This should bring you to a list of nearby taxi ranks.
  5. Select the taxi rank of your choice
  6. After selecting the taxi rank of your choice,a map will display the quickest route.
  7. Follow the given directions and you have reached your destination.
